<section class="section">
  <h2>About</h2>
  <p>
    The TDABench 2026 Workshop convenes researchers and practitioners to create shared
    benchmarks, datasets, and evaluation protocols for Topological Data Analysis (TDA).
    We will focus on reproducibility, fair comparisons across methods, and open-source
    reference implementations.
  </p>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <h2>Goals</h2>
  <div class="grid-3">
    <div class="card"><h3>Benchmark Suite</h3><p>Define tasks, metrics, and baselines across modalities (images, time series, graphs).</p></div>
    <div class="card"><h3>Datasets & Protocols</h3><p>Curate datasets and clear train/val/test splits with robust evaluation protocols.</p></div>
    <div class="card"><h3>Reproducible Pipelines</h3><p>Share containerized reference implementations and CI-validated results.</p></div>
  </div>
</section>
